About/Information

This project brings attention to a very serious issue right now in California. With the massive amounts of coverage on the issue including media, home bloggers, and California Governor Brown’s January declaration of a state of emergency, many California residents are questioning how it is possible that they turn on the faucet, and water flows out. People are counting on a successful 2016 El Niño year to actually bring California out of this drought and supply water for its people, industries, and environment. Given that California is a huge agricultural state and has a massive population and tourist industry, it can be scary when this happens. However, it happens a lot. Many times in the past, a regular cycle of rain, no rain, and then rain again repeats frequently. This project is basically a scientific and mathematical analysis of how things have changed and how things have stayed the same in California during drought years.
